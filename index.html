Reduce Cost & Increase Uptime With IoT-enabled, AI-Powered SaaS Platform

How does Hestalogix's on-ground advisory help fleet owners optimize their operations?

Hestalogix’s on-ground advisory provides hands-on support to fleet owners to optimize their operations by establishing baseline, identifying areas for improvement, implementing tailored solutions, and monitoring progress in real-time. This approach ensures maximum results and cost savings for clients through advanced fleet management solutions and Fuel monitoring solutions.

What asset types can benefit from Hestalogix's solutions?

If your asset operates on fuel—then Hestalogix’s solutions will prove beneficial to your asset by improving the fuel consumption and asset uptime. Our solution is applicable to a wide range of assets like trucks, buses, DG sets, cars, etc., across multiple industries, including telecommunications, mining, construction, fleet operations, etc. Hestalogix’s offering combines a Fuel Management System with fleet tracking capabilities to maximize asset productivity and operational control.

What is fleet management?

Fleet management involves overseeing and coordinating vehicles or vessels for efficient operations. Fleet management solutions help optimize logistics, reduce costs, and track assets in real-time.

How much can a business expect to save on fuel and improve uptime with Hestalogix's solutions?

Amount of fuel savings and improved uptime a business can expect to achieve with Hestalogix’s solutions varies depending on the client’s specific circumstances. However, Hestalogix is committed to delivering upto 15% improvement in mileage and 20%-25% increase in uptime through our advanced fleet management system.
